Current Job Openings

Home Health Aide

Location: West Metro

We are seeking a compassionate and dependable Home Health Aide to provide quality care and support to our clients in their homes. Responsibilities include assisting with daily living activities (such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and meal preparation), monitoring client well-being, offering companionship, and helping to maintain a safe, comfortable environment.

Qualifications: Previous caregiving experience preferred but not required; reliable transportation; strong communication and empathy skills.

Pay rate: $19-$24/hr

Registered Nurse

Location: West Metro

We are looking for a dedicated Registered Nurse to join our care team. Responsibilities include administering medications and treatments, monitoring patient health, documenting progress, and collaborating with doctors, families, and caregivers to ensure high-quality care plans are followed.

Qualifications: Current RN license in Minnesota, strong clinical skills, ability to work independently and as part of a team, and excellent communication skills.

Pay rate: $48-$50/hr
