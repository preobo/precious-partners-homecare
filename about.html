Meet the Founder

Janet Obonyo

Founder & CEO

Precious Partners Homecare was founded by Dr. Janet Obonyo, DNP, AGPCNP, APRN, a compassionate and experienced healthcare provider with over 20 years in the medical field. As a Doctor of Nursing Practice and board-certified Adult-Gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioner, she brings a deep commitment to quality, personalized care for adults and seniors.

Dr. Obonyo has served in leading healthcare systems such as HealthPartners, Park Nicollet, and HCMC, and currently works full-time as a Nurse Practitioner while also teaching as a nursing professor at Herzing University. Her strong foundation in nursing education and hands-on care is the heart of Precious Partners.
